# Executive Summary – Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment

## Objective
Analyze how Bitcoin Fear/Greed sentiment influences trader behavior and performance on Hyperliquid and derive actionable strategies.

## Key Insights
1. Fear days exhibit higher downside risk and volatility than Greed days.
2. High leverage amplifies gains in Greed but causes larger drawdowns in Fear.
3. Frequent traders underperform during Fear regimes.
4. Sentiment alone is a weak predictor; behavior metrics dominate.

## Strategy Rules
- Cap leverage during Fear days unless trader is historically consistent.
- Throttle excessive trade frequency during Fear regimes.
- Apply directional bias only for traders with positive sentiment-conditional expectancy.

## Conclusion
Sentiment should be treated as a risk-modulating signal rather than a standalone trading trigger.
